About GreenHarvest

Our mission, vision, and the team behind sustainable agriculture

Our Story

GreenHarvest was founded in 1985 by John and Martha Green, third-generation farmers who recognized the need for more sustainable farming practices.

What began as a small family consultancy has grown into a leading agricultural solutions provider, helping thousands of farmers across the country transition to more environmentally responsible and economically viable farming methods.

Our journey has been guided by a simple principle: farming should nourish both people and the planet. This belief continues to drive everything we do.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with sustainable solutions that increase productivity while preserving natural resources for future generations.

Our Vision

A world where agriculture enhances rather than depletes the environment, where farmers thrive economically, and where communities have access to nutritious, sustainably grown food.
